Marcus Ross

Writer Director Cinematographer Gimbal Operator Editor

A five time Emmy-winning writer and director from Oklahoma.

Marcus is the owner of Clover Bloom Productions. He just co-produced his first feature film “The Vast of Night.” He wrote, directed, and produced the television comedy pilot “Lazy Circles” in his small hometown of Minco, Oklahoma. “Lazy Circles” is currently making the festival rounds, already winning multiple festival awards.

Marcus graduated with a bachelors degree in Corporate Media from Oklahoma Christian University and studied film at the Los Angeles Film Studies Center.
